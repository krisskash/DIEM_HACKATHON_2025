The Problem We're Solving

Traditional delivery services are expensive, centralized, and take large commission cuts from drivers. We're demonstrating how blockchain can create a fairer, more transparent alternative where 90% of delivery fees go directly to delivery partners.

By using smart contracts, we eliminate the need for trust between strangers. The technology ensures payment is only released when delivery is confirmed, protecting both customers and delivery partners.
